Pioneering in Flexible Packaging Materials

深耕塑料包装与工业原材料行业

Anhui Eastern Progress Import & Export Co., Ltd is an enterprise with independent import and export rights approved by the Administration for Industry and Commerce, registered in Hefei, Anhui Province, China.

安徽东渐进出口有限公司是一家经工商管理局批准、具有自主进出口经营权的综合性对外贸易企业。注册于中国安徽省合肥市。

We started from flexible plastic packaging films and bags. After years of operation, we have grown into a comprehensive trading enterprise with strong advantages in BOPP, BOPET, POF, PE, PVC films, self-adhesive labels, thermal transfer ribbons, packaging machinery and solar panels.

公司从软塑料包装薄膜及制品业起步，经过多年的经营，成长为在包装薄膜（BOPP薄膜、BOPET薄膜、POF热收缩膜、PE薄膜、PVC膜）、薄膜深加工、胶粘带、打印碳带、标签及包装设备行业具有独特优势的综合性对外经贸企业。

With a focus on the "Belt and Road" initiative, we have built a robust client portfolio across Europe, Southeast Asia, Middle East, South America, and Africa, delivering premium and cost-efficient industrial supplies.

我们紧密围绕“一带一路”产业布局，在欧洲、东南亚、中东、南美和非洲市场建立了广泛、庞大的新老客户群，持续提供质优价廉的工业商品与服务。
